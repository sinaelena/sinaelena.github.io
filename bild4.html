BEYOND THE LETTER

The project explores letterforms—focusing on the proportions and shapes of individual letters, both in illustrative and poster-like styles, which will be used to create a bound book. It involves experimenting with new techniques and styles, blending them together while consciously challenging traditional typography norms and graphic design conventions. The goal is to move away from the precision and perfection of digital typography, embracing a more creative and personal approach.
