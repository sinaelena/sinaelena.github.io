CHILDRENS BOOK

ONE DAY

This project presents a children's book that illustrates a full day in caregiving, with a focus on the daily routines and the various times of day to highlight the activities of a 24-hour care shift. The book uses visual elements to depict tasks at specific times, and a red thread running through the pages guides the reader through the entire day, helping to navigate the flow of caregiving tasks from start to finish.
