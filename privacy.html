2. Information We Collect

When you fill out our contact form, we collect the following personal data:

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and use this data solely to respond to your inquiry or message. We will not use your contact information for marketing purposes unless you explicitly consent to that.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your personal data will be used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your inquiry.
  • Address any questions or issues you raise.

We will not share your personal data with any third parties unless required by law or if necessary to process your request (for example, using an email provider for correspondence).

5. Data Retention

We retain the personal data you submit via the contact form only for as long as necessary to respond to your message or inquiry. After that, your data will be deleted unless there are legal obligations to retain it for a longer period.

6. Security of Your Data

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the correction of any incorrect or incomplete personal data.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data, unless we are required to keep it for legal reasons.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at sina.lang@fh-potsdam.de.

8. Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal data with third parties unless required by law or in connection with the use of third-party services (e.g., email hosting). We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising services at this time.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and update the “Last Updated” date below.
