BASIC INTERFACE

DIGITAL PLATFORM FOR DESIGNERS TO CCONNECT App for Designers: This project is about an app designed specifically for designers, serving as a platform where they can connect, share their work, and engage with one another. It’s inspired by Instagram but takes a more minimalist approach, focusing solely on creativity and professional growth. Users can share their projects, participate in design challenges, and build meaningful connections with like-minded individuals. The app eliminates distractions and prioritizes the creative process, making it a dedicated space for designers to showcase their talent, exchange ideas, and grow together. FIGMA.

DIGITAL COOK BOOK

Digital Coffee Barista Guide – A Digital Transformation of an Analog Coffee Book This project involved the creation of a digital book that reimagines a traditional, analog coffee barista guide, transforming it into an interactive and user-friendly digital format. The book covers a wide range of topics, including coffee recipes, the various types of coffee foam, and detailed instructions on how to create the perfect frothy texture for different coffee drinks. In addition to step-by-step guides for crafting specialty coffee, the digital book also provides tips, techniques, and advice for aspiring baristas. It delves into everything from coffee bean selection to the best brewing methods and how to perfect your latte art. A key feature of the project is its exploration of Italy’s rich coffee culture. The digital book offers a deeper insight into the Italian origins of coffee, showcasing famous Italian coffee drinks and traditions. By digitizing this barista guide, the project aimed to make learning about coffee more accessible, interactive, and visually engaging, offering a modern twist on a classic analog guide. FIGMA.

INFOGRAPHIC

SERVICE DESIGN

Project: TenantMatch - A Service Design Solution for Landlords In this UX design project, I focused on creating a service-oriented solution for landlords to simplify and improve the tenant selection process. The result was a website and landing page designed to help landlords find their perfect tenant efficiently and effectively. The platform is supported by an AI-powered chatbot, which guides landlords through the process of filtering potential tenants based on specific criteria such as financial stability, lifestyle preferences, and rental history. The system works like a “rental match” platform, similar to Tinder, but tailored to property management. By streamlining communication and providing smart filtering tools, the platform saves time and ensures a better match between landlords and tenants, creating a more seamless and reliable renting experience. FIGMA.
